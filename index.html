Company Overview

Clinton Companies and Holdings is a diversified enterprise operating in agriculture and stock investment. The company integrates modern farming systems with structured financial investments to drive sustainable wealth creation, food security, and economic growth in Delta State and across Nigeria.

Founded by Samuel Joseph Oluchukwu, the company is supported by 130 investors and 35 shareholders, reflecting strong financial confidence, stability, and long-term growth capacity.
